# Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to our **Gimme Advice** app (“the app”).

The app does not collect personal identifiers, such as names, addresses, phone numbers, or email addresses.

The app does collect limited data solely for the purpose of serving targeted advertisements:
Device and browser information (e.g., browser type, version, and language)
Non-personalized user behavior data (e.g., clicks, impressions, and engagement)

This data is used to deliver targeted ads based on user interests and preferences. The app does not use this data for any other purpose.

The app does not disclose any collected data to third parties except:
To trusted advertising partners for the purpose of serving targeted ads
As required by law or legal proceedings

By accessing and using the app, you agree to this privacy policy and consent to the collection of limited data for advertising purposes.

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us at [privacy@harricode.dev](mailto:privacy@harricode.dev).

We reserve the right to update this privacy policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting. Your continued use of our app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
